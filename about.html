About Us

FoundersKE is a Kenyan based company that is focused on supporting and mentoring enthusiastic young people to become successful entrepreneurs.

We are a team of young people who are passionate about entrepreneurship and are committed to helping young people to become successful entrepreneurs.

Using industry experts, we have developed a platform & system that will help young people to learn about entrepreneurship and to develop their entrepreneurial skills.

The objective lies in turning ideas into reality and helping young people to initiate and launch their start-ups as entrepreneurs.

This objective is supported by our Vision, Mission & Values.

Our Vision

Mentoring young creates towards effectively redefining & recreating their future

Our Mission

Life is good, & time gives us the power to implement our creativity

Our Values

2* Integrity
3* Teamwork
